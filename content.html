Content Development

AK Horizon have a dedicated team of subject matter experts for various writing services that includes original item Creation(Creating Questions and Answer), TextBook Solutions (using Latex, Mathtype,etc.), Video Solutions, item Cloning, item alignment and items review. Items (Questions And Answers) can be delivered in word document, professional writing software or as per the client requirement such as insertion in their CMS(content Management System).

Quality Assurance

We assured the best quality as per the client standard by giving proper training and continuous monitoring of quality. Our quality team review the content before delivering it to client and assure the best quality. Some key aspects in the review include

   Alignment to standards(State,Common Core, Proprietary) or content.
   Subject and scenario accurary.
   Grade appropriateness(scenario, subject, language).
   Distractor design and rationales.
   Bias.
   Difficulty levels and thinking skills.
   Writing guidelines and art specifications.
