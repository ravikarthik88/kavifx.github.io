How Do We Define Retirement Plan Success?

A successful retirement is one where you...

Live Out Life in Your Home

Age at home in comfortable surroundings instead of in a nursing home, hospital, or other health care facility

Avoid Becoming a Burden

Get the support you need without relying on unpaid family caregivers to sacrifice their lives to care for yours.

Have Money Left

You have a plan to pay for long-term care that will preserve assets so you can enjoy greater quality of life.

Bridge the Gaps that Lead to Retirement Plan Failure

Today you have a 7 in 10 chance of failing in retirement - despite the plans you already have in place. That is because there are gaps hidden in the common approach to retirement.

LifePlanning makes it possible for individuals, families, and the professionals who guide them to create highly effective retirement plans that seamlessly coordinate the five most important variables in the aging equation: health, housing, finance, legal, and family issues.
